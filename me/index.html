Hi, I'm Karen! I'm a non-traditional student studying computer science with a background in pharmacy and pharmaceutical sciences.

My skills include: being able to work on and lead teams (remote and in-person), research, problem solving and customer service.

I really enjoy taking part in and planning hackathons. I have participated in 3 hackathons, and have won awards for 2.

I'm currently part of the OSU Hackathon Club's leadership team (as President & Webmaster), having successfully planned BeaverHacks Summer 2020, BeaverHacks Fall 2020, and BeaverHacks Winter 2021.

I'm working on hosting programming workshops for OSU students because I want to make programming and CS more accessible for everyone, regardless of major or background.

Outside of school, I'm working on exploring web development, mobile app development, HCI, UX design, and IoT (Arduino and Raspberry Pi).
